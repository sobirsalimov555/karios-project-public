Understanding VaR

Value at Risk (VaR) = The maximum loss expected over a given time period at a given confidence level.
95% VaR of -12% means there is a 5% chance your portfolio loses more than 12% in one quarter.
This simulation models BTC and Oil price paths using historical volatility (Gaussian random walks) and accounts for your BTC holdings and oil expense exposure from your Business Profile.
